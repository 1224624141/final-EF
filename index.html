About

Serving the Minnesota Twin Cities music community since 1968. National Lampoon magazine singled the store out for having the worst name for a business. Be that as it may, it’s a name people don’t forget. Electric Fetus sells everything you're looking for. Music of all genres and formats, including records, CDs, cassettes. As well as DVDs, books, posters, clothing and trinkets.

Record Store Day

Record Store Day, typically held once a year on the third Saturday of April, celebrates the culture of the independently owned record stores. Each independent record store hosts their own events and promotions, with special edition releases and sometimes even meet and greets from well respected bands or musicians. Record Store Day is on April 20th, 2024.
